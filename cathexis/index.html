Your Private Panic Attack Companion

Cathexis is a mental health app designed specifically for people experiencing panic attacks and acute anxiety. With a panic-first design philosophy, the app provides instant access to proven grounding techniques, breathing exercises, truth cards, and SOS mode—tools that help you regain control during overwhelming moments.

Unlike generic wellness apps, Cathexis is built for crisis moments—when you need immediate, effective support. Every feature is accessible with minimal taps, works completely offline, and prioritizes your privacy above all else. Core features are free; an optional Premium tier (Cathexis Pro) adds custom themes, alternate app icons, and extra customization.

SOS Mode

One tap starts a guided sequence: breathing, haptics, calming messages, and optional voice-over. Check-ins and quick access to emergency contacts when you need more help.

Breathe

Multiple proven patterns including:

  • Box Breathing (4-4-4-4)
  • Extended Exhale (4-6 pattern)
  • 3-2-3 Method
  • Customizable cycles to match your needs

Configurable from the home screen.

Truth Cards & Affirmations

  • 15+ pre-written reassurance cards
  • Create custom cards with personal affirmations
  • Spaced-repetition practice system to build mental resilience
  • Quick access during panic attacks

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Technique

Interactive sensory awareness exercise in a dedicated hub that reconnects you with the present moment by identifying things you see, touch, hear, smell, and taste.

Sleep

  • Sleep Sounds: High-quality nature soundscapes with sleep timers

Journal (Private Attack Logging)

Track patterns and triggers with detailed logs including:

  • Intensity levels (1-10 scale)
  • Duration tracking
  • Trigger identification
  • Intervention effectiveness
  • Notes and observations
  • All data encrypted and stored locally

Play – Rest & Distraction

  • Bubble Pop Game: Infinite, stress-free distraction to calm your mind

Learn

Understanding panic and anxiety through psychoeducation—so you can build lasting skills.

Premium (Cathexis Pro)

Optional subscription: custom themes, alternate app icons, and extra customization. The free tier includes all core features.

Highly Customizable

  • 3 theme modes (Light, Dark, Bedtime)
  • 5 font families for readability
  • Adjustable haptic feedback intensity
  • Personalized settings for your comfort

Medical Disclaimer

Cathexis is a general wellness tool, not a medical device. It does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment. If you're experiencing a medical emergency, call emergency services (911, 999, 112) immediately.
